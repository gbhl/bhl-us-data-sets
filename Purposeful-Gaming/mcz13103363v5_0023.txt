Friday, February 5, 1869.
  Cloudy & cold
Ther 14 - 22 - 18%
In the house all
day.
  The sleighing is
now very good although
there is but little snow.
  Dr. Wyman called
in the morning to see
fathers face & told me
that I had the
Influenza.
  Henshaw called
in the afternoon to see
me -
Saturday, February 6, 1869.
  Clear and beautiful
day for the season.
Ther. 22 32 18
  In the house again all
day.
  Theo came in the P.M.
but I could not study
my eyes were too weak.
