Wednesday, September 15, 1869.
Clear and still pleasant.
Ther. about 68[degrees] - 75[degrees] - 
68[degrees]. Rose as usual.
Spent the day in
the house, reading 
and otherwise.
Thursday, September 16, 1869.
  Clear and pleasant.
Ther. about 65[degrees] - 70[degrees] - 
67[degrees]. Rose as usual.
Spent the day in
the house. Do not seem
to be much better.
Have the doctor ever
day, who gives me
laudnum, Dover powder
and other drugs.
