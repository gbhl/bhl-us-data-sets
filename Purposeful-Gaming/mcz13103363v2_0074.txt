Memoranda.
Barred Owl. Nothing but
mice
Great northern Shrik. The
remains of a small bird
including the bill.
Common Crossbill. Principally
the seeds of pines.
Mottled Owl. The gizzard
swelled almost to bursting
with nothing but mice
July 18th Long eared Owl. The crop
stuffed out with the 
skulls, bones, & fur of mice
and it contained nothing
else.
Ruffed Grouse Oct '68.
Crop distended with
large acorns.
