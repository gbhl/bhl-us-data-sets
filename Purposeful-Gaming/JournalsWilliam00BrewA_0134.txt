1890
May 28
Cambridge, Massachusetts
under a large willow. Another Green Heron came out of the
Maple Swamp as we were passing the swimming place 
on Glacialis. Just before we reached the Turnpike I heard
a Gallinule cry out, apparently in the marsh on the W. side
of Glacialis.
  There were a few White-bellied and Bank Swallows and
quite a number of Barn Swallows flying over these
marshes to-day.
