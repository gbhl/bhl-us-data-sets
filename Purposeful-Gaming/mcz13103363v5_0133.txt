Monday, September 13, 1869.
Clear and a beautiful
day. Ther. about 65[degrees] - 
75[degrees] - 68[degrees]. Rose as
usual. Spent the
day in the house but
did not suffer so
much as yesterday.
Tuesday, September 14, 1869.
Clear and lovely.
Ther. about 65[degrees] - 74[degrees] - 
68[degrees]. Rose as usual.
Confined to the house
all day.
