1890
Oct. 4
  Cloudy during forenoon with light steady rain. Afternoon
clear. No wind.
  Spent the day about camp. Saw only a few small
birds, much fewer than on previous days. In the afternoon
took the steamer for Lakeside where I met J.C. Melvin
in the evening.
