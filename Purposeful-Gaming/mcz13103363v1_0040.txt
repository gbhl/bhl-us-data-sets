  List of the different
species of birds seen
by R. Deane at Shelbourne
in N. H. from Aug. 8th 
till Aug. 29th 1865
  Cherry birds rather 
abundant. Found
one nest with eggs
and another with
full grown young
ones. They were occasionally
in flocks.
  Gold-finches not numerous.
  Song sparrows in considerable
numbers
  Catbirds not very
plentiful
  Purple Finches
  Saw more females than
males.
  Barn Swallows
not plentiful.
