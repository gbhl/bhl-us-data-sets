Sunday, August 8, 1869.
  Clear and a very
pleasant day. Ther.
about 60[degrees] - 70[degrees] - 60.
Rose late and shaved.
After breakfast walked
down to Garnet Pool
with Ed. French
and stayed there
several hours.
Wrote most of this
P.M. and evening.
Monday, August 9, 1869.
  Clear and warm.
Ther. about 60[degrees] - 75[degrees] - 
65[degrees]. Rose as usual.
After breakfast took
a walk down to Garnet
pool with Allis Luvkin.
Got back at 11.30 A.M.
and set to work on the
four birds left over from
Saturday and finished
in addition a Trochilus
Colubris that I found
this A.M.
In P.M. went down
to the mill pond
fishing & took with
the fly only seven
fish during the whole
afternoon and most of
those small.
