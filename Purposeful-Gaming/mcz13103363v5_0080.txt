Sunday, May 30, 1869.
  Cloudy & rained most
of the day. Ther. about
70[degrees] - 70[degrees] - 60[degrees]. Rose
at about 9 A.M.
On the way down to
church called at the
Horsford's & left a basket.
Walked down with J.
Nichols. In P.M. went
up to see Maynard with
Mr. Davenport & brought
him back with us in
the Rockaway. He looked
over my birds & corrected
several errors of mine
in regard to them.
Wrote most of the
evening.
Monday, May 31, 1869.
  Cloudy & rained all the
morning. Cleared up
in P.M. Ther. about 60[degrees] - 
75[degrees] - 70[degrees]. Rose and
usual and after breakfast
walked down to Mr. Auburn
& took the car to school but
got late by about half-an-
hour. Wrote a Latin Grammar
paper and got out a
little before school closed.
Studied hard all P.M.
In the evening took a
walk with Mr. Davenport &
at 8 P.M. rode over to see
Mr. Barnes about Billy
who is sick. After
getting home gave Billy
a dose of sweet oil.
