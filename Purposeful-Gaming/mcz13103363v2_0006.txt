Saturday, February 3, 1866.
  Saw the first blue jay today:
saw the first robins about twenty
in all and shot one: Saw three
shore larks and shot two
both of which with the robin
I have stuffed. (Shore larks were
shot where Ca[?]dy Hos. now stands)
Sunday, February 4, 1866.
  Five or six robins were seen
to day.
