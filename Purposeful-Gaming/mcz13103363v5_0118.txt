Saturday, August 14, 1869.
  Clear but rather
cool. Ther. about 60[degrees] - 
70[degrees] - 60[degrees]. Rose as
usual. After breakfast
packed my birds and
cleaned my gun.
In the P.M. packed
my butterflies and
other possessions.
Spent the latter part
of the P.M. on the
mill pond and took
sixteen good fish,
all with the fly.
Spent the evening about
the hotel as usual.
The Wymanm party
left this morning.
Sunday, August 15, 1869.
  Clear and cool.
Ther. about 60[degrees] - 65[degrees] - 
60[degrees]. Rained most
of the A.M.
Spent the forenoon
in the house, reading.
Late in P.M. took
a walk down to Garnett
pool and got quite
wet coming home.
