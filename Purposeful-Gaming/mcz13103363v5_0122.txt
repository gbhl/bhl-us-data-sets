Sunday, August 22, 1869.
  Clear & very cool with
an Easterly wind.
Ther. about 55[degrees] - 65[degrees] - 
60[degrees]. Rose as usual.
In A.M. went over
to the other end of the
beach & saw Tom
Philbrick.
Bathed as usual but
Dan did not go in.
Surf good and water
remarkedly warm
for the time.
Wrote most of P.M.
In evening went over
to the Farragut house
as usual.
Monday, August 23, 1869.
  Clear and cold. Ther.
about 58[degrees] - 68[degrees] - 60[degrees].
Rose at 4.45 A.M.
and went out on
the marsh but nothing
came in during the
morning. In A.M.
bathed as usual.
In P.M. went into
the woods but shot
nothing during the
whole afternoon, but
a Crow, which I
killed.
In evening went
over to the Farragut
house as usual.
